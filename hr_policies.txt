Leave Policy: Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave annually.
Maternity Leave Policy: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of maternity leave.
Work From Home Policy: Employees may work from home up to 2 days a week.
Overtime Policy: Overtime will be compensated as per company guidelines.
Probation Policy: New employees will be on a 3-month probation period.
Payroll Policy: Salary is paid monthly on the last working day.
Sick Leave Policy: Employees are entitled to 12 sick leave days per year.
